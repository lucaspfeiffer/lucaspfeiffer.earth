Privacy Policy

Dawn and Dusk does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app uses your device's location services solely to calculate sunrise and sunset times for your current location. This location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to any servers.

Weather information is fetched from Apple's WeatherKit API, which does not require or receive any personal information. Your location coordinates are used to retrieve local weather data, but this information is not stored or used for any other purpose.

The app does not use cookies, analytics, or any form of user tracking. Dawn and Dusk was designed to maintain privacy and only access the minimum required information to provide the core functionality of sunrise, sunset, and weather information.
